About me

My name is Petar Stosic. Serbian-based photographer.What motivates me above all else, is to try and ‘involve’ the viewer in the image – as though the viewer is seeing what I'm seeing. It’s this ‘caught in the moment’ style that differentiates my work, making it almost tangible. Much like the athletes I often shoots, I (a crossfit enthusiast) pushes himself hard and applies the same intensity, industry and endeavor to all my projects
